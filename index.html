01

About

My research sits at the intersection of data-centric machine learning, efficient model training, and visual intelligence.

My recent work asks a simple question: beyond selecting better data, how can we organize data to make learning more effective? This direction spans scoring, selection, ordering, and curriculum design for language-model training.

I also work on efficient computer vision and 3D scene understanding, including dataset pruning, low-data recognition, neural rendering, Gaussian splatting, and large-scale collaborative reconstruction.
