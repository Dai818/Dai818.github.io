AI Researcher · Nanyang Technological University, Singapore

I work on data-centric machine learning, spatial intelligence, and efficient computer vision. My recent research studies how training data can be selected and organized more effectively, and how visual systems can reason about 3D scenes.

Research interests: Data-centric AI, language model training, spatial reasoning, 3D vision.
